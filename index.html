Welcome! My name is Tianjia Huang and I am a fourth years Ph.D student of petroleum engineering at Texas A&M University (Advisor: Prof. George Moridis and Prof. Thomas Blasingame)

My research focuses on numerical simulation of subsurface energy, such as geothermal, hydrogen storage, CCUS, and nuclear waste disposal. I am also interested in developing high-performance reservoir simulator, using MPI, OpenMP, and CUDA.

Education

  • M.E. Mechanical Engineering at Chinese Academy of Sciences
  • B.E. Building Environmental and Energy Application Engineering at Xian Jiaotong University
